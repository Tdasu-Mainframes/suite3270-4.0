stats Indication

Periodic I/O statistics. These will be sent no more than once every two seconds, and only if there is a change.

AttributeAlways present?Purpose
bytes-receivedyesBytes received on this connection
records-receivedyesRecords received on this connection
bytes-sentyesBytes sent on this connection
records-sentyesRecords send on this connection

Example: 

<stats bytes-received="1343" records-received="4" bytes-sent="32" records-sent="0"/>
