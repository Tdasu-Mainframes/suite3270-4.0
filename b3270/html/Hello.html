hello Indication

Initialization message. It is the first indication nested within the initialize element.
AttributeAlways present?Purpose
versionyesVersion number, e.g., 3.6.1
buildyesBuild string (timestamp)
copyrightyesCopyright statement

Example: 

<hello version="3.6.1" build="b3270 v3.6alpha1 Mon May 30 10:06:18 CDT 2016 pdm" copyright="Copyright...">
